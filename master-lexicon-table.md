## Recommendations for Use  
1. **Main copy / headlines:** Use the **full Spanish term** — e.g., *“Proveedores de salud independientes”*.  
2. **Social posts / short messaging:** Use the **punchy variant** — e.g., *“Consultorios modernos”*, *“Proveedores comunitarios”*.  
3. **Avoid “pequeño” / “small”** entirely — it signals weakness or limitation.  
4. **Mix categories depending on context:**  
    * Trust / local: Community & Local Focus  
    * Professional / authority: Ownership & Independence  
    * Innovation / growth: Growth & Innovation-Oriented  
    * Premium / specialization: Premium / Specialized / Personalized
