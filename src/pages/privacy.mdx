## 1. Introduction and Scope

Futurion Solutions S.L. ("Futurion", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to
protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect
personal data when you visit our website (https://solutions.futurion.es), submit a contact or
Automation Roadmap form, or otherwise interact with us as a prospective or existing client.

This Policy covers our **website and commercial relationship with you**. It does not cover personal
data that our clients process using our products (TrustPrompt, TrustCore, TrustAuto) on their own
infrastructure — see [§12, Products and Client Data](#12-products-and-client-data) below.

We process personal data in accordance with:

- Regulation (EU) 2016/679, the General Data Protection Regulation ("**GDPR**")
- Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on Data Protection and Digital Rights Guarantee ("**LOPDGDD**")
- Law 34/2002 on Information Society Services and Electronic Commerce ("**LSSI-CE**"), for cookies
  and commercial electronic communications

## 2. Definitions

- **Personal data:** any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- **Controller:** the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data —
  in this Policy, Futurion Solutions S.L.
- **Processor:** an entity that processes personal data on behalf of the controller (e.g. our
  hosting or email provider).
- **Processing:** any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, disclosure,
  deletion, etc.).
- **Data subject:** the individual whose personal data is processed (you).
- **Website:** https://solutions.futurion.es.
- **Cookies:** small files or similar technologies stored on your device (see [§6](#6-cookies-and-tracking-technologies)).

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website uses a cookie consent banner that lets you accept or reject each category before it is
loaded, in line with LSSI-CE Art. 22.2:

- **Essential cookies:** required for the Website to function (e.g. theme and consent preference).
  These do not require consent.
- **Analytics cookies:** Google Analytics 4, used only with your consent, to understand aggregate
  usage of the Website.
- **Marketing cookies:** the Meta Pixel and the LinkedIn Insight Tag, used only with your consent, to
  measure and improve our advertising.

You can change or withdraw your cookie consent at any time through the cookie preferences control on
the Website, or through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Website
functionality.

Our Website hosting and contact-form data are kept within the European Economic Area (EEA) (AWS EU
region), and our business email and appointment booking are processed by Microsoft 365 in Microsoft's

## 9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

- **Contact form and Automation Roadmap submissions:** up to 12 months after your last interaction,
  unless you become a client.
- **Client data:** for the duration of our business relationship, plus the retention periods required
  by Spanish commercial and tax law (generally up to 6 years for accounting records).
- **Website analytics data:** up to 14 months, in line with Google Analytics' default retention.

You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time — see [§11](#11-your-rights-under-gdpr).

## 11. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights, free of charge, under GDPR Articles 15–22:

- **Right of access (Art. 15):** request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Right to rectification (Art. 16):** request correction of inaccurate personal data
- **Right to erasure (Art. 17):** request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to restrict processing (Art. 18):** request limitation of how we process your data
- **Right to data portability (Art. 20):** receive your data in a structured, commonly used,
  machine-readable format
- **Right to object (Art. 21):** object to processing based on our legitimate interest, including
  direct marketing
- **Right to withdraw consent:** withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of
  processing carried out before withdrawal

To exercise any of these rights, please use our [contact form](/contact/). We will respond within one
month of your request, as required by GDPR Art. 12(3).

## 12. Products and Client Data

TrustPrompt, TrustCore, and TrustAuto are designed to run on infrastructure that our clients control.
When a client uses our products to process personal data of their own users, patients, or employees,
that data stays on the client's infrastructure and **the client is the data controller** for that
processing. Where Futurion processes such data on the client's behalf (for example, during
deployment, support, or maintenance), that processing is governed by a separate data processing
agreement (GDPR Art. 28) with the client, not by this Policy.
